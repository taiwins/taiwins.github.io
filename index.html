Taiwins is a modern and modular wayland compositor, and it is extensible through lua script. It has a builtin shell and GUI system called twclient. Writing a minimum wayland client using twclient would take only 20 lines of code.

Taiwins is the tiling as well as a floating window manager, you can allocate windows on your desktop logicially or based on your needs, drag your windows freely.
